Julian Mak's homepage

I am currently an assistant professor (cf. lecturer in the UK system) in the Department of Ocean Sciences (OCES) at HKUST (Hong Kong University of Science and Technology). I am generally interested in fluid dynamical questions relevant to geophysical and astrophysical systems, and have expertise in using/developing numerical models and inverse methods.

Beyond the page groupings, my contact details may be found under ABOUT, and a calendar of my schedule may be found under TRAVEL.
